Timeline

A progression of Skill Over Time

Ravenfield: Dogfight (July 28, 2017)

I started game development in Unity 3D by making downloadable maps for "Ravenfield", a popular PC indie game. "Dogfight" was a huge hit, recieving 25,000 unique subscribers. This sparked my interest in making games in Unity 3D. 

Solo Royale (September 27, 2021)

After making a few smaller projects that never saw the light of day, I decided to learn how to make a standalone game. I created "Solo Royale" using a collection of different assets. This was helpful for me to learn how to make a standalone game by not starting with an intimidating blank screen. 

Baseball (Feburary 16, 2022)

"Baseball" was my next finished product. It was my first game that I completed completely from scratch. No help from premade assets this time!

Squid Guys (Feburary 28, 2022)

After gaining some more programming confidence and momentum, I wanted to see how similiar I could make a game to "Fall Guys". I used a collection of assets inlcluding "Puppet Master", an active ragdoll asset pack with navigational capabilities for animated humanoids.

Warfield (Coming Soon)

My first attempt at a detailed Third Person Shooter. It features a conquest campaign gamemode.
